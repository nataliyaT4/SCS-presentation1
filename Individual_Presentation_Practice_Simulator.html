Modernity: institutions, expertise, professionalisation, standardisation and administration.
Postmodernity: contested truth, plural narratives, representation, identity and institutional authority.
Risk society: manufactured risks, uncertainty, unequal exposure, global consequences and institutional responsibility.

A strong answer uses PREP+

  1. Point: answer the question immediately.
  2. Reason: name the principle or criterion.
  3. Evidence: refer to the case or theory.
  4. Perspective: bring in the second discipline.
  5. Qualification: acknowledge limits without abandoning the judgement.
